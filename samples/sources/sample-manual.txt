# Sample Equipment Manual

Intended use: The BRX-500 bioreactor is designed for sterile cell culture operations in GMP facilities.

Core functions include mixing, heating, cooling, and agitation.

Utilities: steam, purified water, compressed air, and electrical power.

Safety features: emergency stop, alarm management, and software interlock handling.

Sensors and Interfaces: temperature, pressure, flow, and pH sensors with OPC and Ethernet data interfaces.

Software version: BRX Control v3.1.4.

Process ranges:
- Temperature 2 to 8 C
- Pressure 1 to 2 bar
